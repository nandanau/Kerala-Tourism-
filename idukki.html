IDUKKI!!

Idukki, situated in the heart of Kerala, India, is a district renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty and diverse topography. Home to the majestic Idukki Dam, one of the largest arch dams in Asia, the district is a haven for hydroelectric power generation. Its picturesque hill stations like Munnar and Vagamon offer panoramic views of rolling hills, lush tea estates, and cascading waterfalls. Idukki is also known for its rich biodiversity, with numerous wildlife sanctuaries and national parks, including the famous Periyar Tiger Reserve, showcasing the region's vibrant flora and fauna. The district's serene backwaters, tranquil lakes, and spice plantations make it an ideal destination for eco-tourism and adventure enthusiasts alike.Founded: 1972
